About Me

Hi, I’m Erika, a tech professional with a passion for problem-solving and bringing ideas to life through technology. My career journey began in health sciences, but my curiosity and love for innovation led me to pivot into tech. I joined a software development bootcamp where I sharpened my skills in programming languages like JavaScript, Java, and SQL, and discovered my knack for creating scalable and efficient solutions.

I’ve had the chance to work on projects that push me to grow as a developer, building both front-end and back-end systems. I genuinely enjoy taking concepts and turning them into functional, user-friendly applications that make an impact.

When I’m not coding, I’m likely exploring new technologies, diving into personal projects (such as this page!), or learning something new to stay ahead in the fast-moving tech world. Outside of work, you can find me hiking with my family or cooking, which keeps me balanced and inspired.

I’d love to connect—whether it’s to collaborate, chat about the latest in tech, or share ideas. Let’s build something great together!
