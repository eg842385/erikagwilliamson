About Me

Hi, I’m Erika, a tech professional with a passion for turning raw data into actionable insights and building innovative solutions. My journey started in health sciences, but my love for problem-solving and curiosity led me to pivot into the fields of data analysis and software development.

I’ve honed my skills in data analysis, SQL, and Power BI, as well as programming languages like JavaScript and Java. From designing data-driven dashboards to developing full-stack applications, I thrive on creating scalable, efficient, and impactful solutions that drive decision-making.

Some of my favorite projects have challenged me to combine technical precision with creativity, whether it’s building intuitive user interfaces or analyzing complex datasets to uncover trends. I enjoy working at the intersection of data and technology to deliver value and make a tangible impact.

Outside of work, you’ll likely find me exploring new technologies, diving into personal projects (like this page!), or staying curious as a lifelong learner. On the rare occasion I step away from my computer, I’m hiking with my family, brewing the perfect cup of coffee, or experimenting in the kitchen. Let’s connect and build something impactful together!
